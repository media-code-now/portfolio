About Us

In a nutshell

YOUR PROJECT IS IN SAFE HANDS

NSM Prime was established in 2017 by two passionate euntreprenuers who decided to step up and do what they love. We are a marketing and advertising agency located in Las Vegas, Nevada. We have social media specialists, graphic designers, web developers and everything you need to build a successful online platform. What started as a two-men shop has grown into a full-service digital marketing agency.

We’re fueled by data, collaboration, and the passion to tell brand stories. We use this fuel to drive digital strategy, design award-winning businesses, and surpass client goals. Our team of experts specialize in content creation, media buying, digital and social strategy and influencer marketing.

Our Creative Process

1

Discuss The Project

Our people-centered approach allows us to identify the talent that is best suited for our clients’ projects. We have a thorough selection process that evaluates technical skills, soft skills, and communication in order to guarantee that our project delivers as expected.

2

Brainstorming Concept

With our brainstorm activity we will share ideas, run projects and finalize a plan to move forward with a customized strategy to your own needs.

3

Market Research

The process of gathering information about a market within an industry. Our analysis studies the dynamics of a market and what makes potential customers tick. A market analysis may seem complex, but it’s necessary if you want to lead your business in the direction of success.

4

UI/UX Design

The user interface is the graphical layout of an application. It consists of the buttons users click on, the text they read, the images, sliders, text entry fields, and all the rest of the items the user interacts with. This includes screen layout, transitions, interface animations and every single micro-interaction. Any sort of visual element, interaction, or animation must be designed and detailed.

5

Visual Design

Our visual design team aims to improve the product's appeal and usability with suitable images, typography, space, layout and color. It is about more than just aesthetics. Designers place elements carefully to create interfaces that optimize user experience and drive conversion.

6

Interaction

This is the final step of our process. Now your platform is ready and we release it to the public so that you can start focusing on what really matters - your clients. Once the site is up, we will maintain the website with relevant content in order to assure maxumim optimization.
